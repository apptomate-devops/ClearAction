Marketers' Strengths:

Marketers are masters of quick changes in a fast-paced environment. Beyond that, changes that require new outlooks or habits – especially among a group of people – can make the best of us feel like a fish out of water. Fortunately, "change management" thinking is a lot like planning a marketing campaign: who's affected, what's in it for them, how to get their attention, and how to build engagement.

Approach:

To manage others’ adoption of change:

  1. Identify the roles they need to play,
  2. assess “what’s in it for me” from their perspective,
  3. create a change playbook to guide your interactions with them, and
  4. leverage early adopters of change to bring along the others.
