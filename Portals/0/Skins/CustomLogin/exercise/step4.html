STEP4

Leverage Early Adopters

Wisdom for this step:

To accelerate market adoption of what your company sells, you will probably want to engage early adopters in helping to spread your message. The same is true in leading a big change. Think about which other stakeholders they have an opportunity to influence. Then brainstorm ways they could be a good influence, and ways you could help them get started as an influencer.

72

Emphasize the Positive

Identify any stakeholder who is a believer in the change. Engage them as part of your playbook for reducing obstacle.

1

What’s appealing to Product Management:

For Product Management: High NPS scores that are tied to increased business outcomes (e.g. revenue growth)

2

What’s appealing to Corporate Marketing:

Increasing brand affinity and rankings across the Analyst community.

3

What’s appealing to Customer Experience Team:

High NPS scores that are tied to increased business outcomes (e.g. revenue growth)

4

What’s appealing to Marketing Operations:

Integrated KPI's that align cross-functional priorities.
