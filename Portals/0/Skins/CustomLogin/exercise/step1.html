STEP1

Identify Needed Roles

Wisdom for this step:

In planning a marketing campaign it's best to clearly identify your target audience and get to know their needs before creating marketing messages. It's the same for any big change from people's routines. Use these templates for every effort that requires your stakeholders to get onboard with a new way of doing things.

Scenario:

Kelly is leading a task force to integrate communications across the company’s divisions for events, ads, media relations, and all other communications. Decentralized management has led to inconsistencies and duplication of resources. After the first meeting, Kelly realized that there are some strong opinions in the group, and it will be challenging to get everyone “on the same page.
