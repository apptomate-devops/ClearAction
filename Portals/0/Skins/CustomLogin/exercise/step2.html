STEP2

Assess What’s in it for Them

Wisdom for this step:

Discovering your target audience's hot buttons is a key to successful messaging, both in marketing campaigns and in managing a group's transition to new habits. Understand what makes them tick and emphasize "what's in it for them" early and often.

72

For each stakeholder, make a note about what’s not appealing to them, and what is appealing to them.

Tips

When considering what’s appealing and not appealing, think about things like overall company objectives, as well as their personal objectives. Things that enable or inhibit progress. Things that customers recognize and will appreciate, or not. Long and short-term effects on customer experience.

Tips

When looking for patterns, consider things like:

  • Understanding the common metrics across each of your stakeholders.
  • Is there a common customer outcome that all your stakeholders can identify with — what matters most to your customers?
  • Are we talking to the right market segments?
