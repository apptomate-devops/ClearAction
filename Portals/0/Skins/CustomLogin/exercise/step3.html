Create a Change Playbook

Wisdom for this step:

In a marketing campaign, once your target audience's persona is defined, you are ready to create messaging and a portfolio of integrated marketing communications. Likewise, in managing a change, you are now ready to create a playbook of messages and interactions that will (1) reduce what's not appealing and (2) increase what is appealing for each of your stakeholders.
