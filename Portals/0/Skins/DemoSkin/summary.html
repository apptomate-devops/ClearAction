Identify Needed Roles

Wisdom for this step:

In planning a marketing campaign it's best to clearly identify your target audience and get to know their needs before creating marketing messages. It's the same for any big change from people's routines. Use these templates for every effort that requires your stakeholders to get onboard with a new way of doing things.

How might this topic be applied to your work?

Need to get two different business units to deﬁne a common set of requirements and approach to serving the CX needs of all customers while accommodating different approaches for different audiences and customer segments

Brainstorm a list of all the people (or at least all the groups/departments) who have a stake in the change. Indicate the role needed by them for the success of the change — Let It Happen, Help It Happen, or Make It Happen.

Assess What's in it for Me

Wisdom for this step:

Discovering your target audience's hot buttons is a key to successful messaging, both in marketing campaigns and in managing a group's transition to new habits. Understand what makes them tick and emphasize "what's in it for them" early and often.

Assess What’s in it for Me

For each stakeholder, make a note about what’s not appealing to them, and what is appealing to them.

Create a Change Playbook

Wisdom for this step:

In a marketing campaign, once your target audience's persona is deﬁned, you are ready to create messaging and a portfolio of integrated marketing communications. Likewise, in managing a change, you are now ready to create a playbook of messages and interactions that will (1) reduce what's not appealing and (2) increase what is appealing for each of your stakeholders.

Leverage Early Adopters

Wisdom for this step:

To accelerate market adoption of what your company sells, you will probably want to engage early adopters in helping to spread your message. The same is true in leading a big change. Think about which other stakeholders they have an opportunity to inﬂuence. Then brainstorm ways they could be a good inﬂuence, and ways you could help them get started as an inﬂuencer.
