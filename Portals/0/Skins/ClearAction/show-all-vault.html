  • Is What They're Buying What You're Marketing?

  • Marketing that Invites — Not Invades

    Marketers use buyer personas to tailor efforts for the best results, yet many buyers have the impression that a great percentage of marketing is still invasive in its timing, delivery, messaging or engagement.   • How Customer-Centered is Your Group?

    Marketers are always thinking about customers. Even so, it can be easy to take customers for granted or strive to shape or change customers in company-serving ways.   • Speak the Language of Your Stakeholders

    Marketers are experienced in creating compelling messages to customers, but typically have less emphasis on convincing non-customer stakeholders of insights and needs. Learn the forces on each of your stakeholders so your conversations, requests, and advice are more likely to be viewed by them as relevant and compelling.Completed 10/3/2017

  • Mastering Questions to Illuminate & Motivate
